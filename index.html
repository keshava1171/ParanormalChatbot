What is the Paranormal?

The term "paranormal" refers to events, phenomena, or experiences that lie beyond the scope of scientific understanding. This includes ghost sightings, hauntings, poltergeists, and other supernatural occurrences that challenge our understanding of the physical world.

People have reported paranormal experiences for centuries, and these mysterious events continue to captivate our imagination and curiosity. Some believe in spirits and energies that transcend the living world, while others remain skeptical.
